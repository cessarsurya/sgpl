Formula

θ = τ₀ / [(ρₛ - ρ) × g × d]

  • θ: Shield Parameter
  • τ₀: Shear stress (\( τ₀ = ρ uₐ² \))
  • ρ: Water density
  • ρₛ: Sediment density
  • g: Gravitational acceleration (9.81 m/s²)
  • d: Sediment diameter
